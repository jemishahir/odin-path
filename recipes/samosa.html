Samosas recipe


here's a simple recipe for delicious samosas:

    Ingredients:
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon coriander seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 cup mashed potatoes
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine flour and salt. Add in vegetable oil and mix well until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Gradually add water and knead until you have a smooth and pliable dough.
  2. Cover the dough and let it rest for at least 30 minutes.
  3. While the dough is resting, prepare the filling. Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a pan and add cumin seeds and coriander seeds. Cook until fragrant.
  4. Add turmeric, garam masala, cumin powder, coriander powder, and red chili powder to the pan. Stir to combine.
  5. Add mashed potatoes and frozen peas to the pan and stir well. Cook for 5-7 minutes or until the mixture is heated through. Remove from heat and let it cool.
  6. Preheat oil in a deep fryer or a heavy-bottomed pan over medium heat.
  7. Divide the dough into small balls and roll each ball into a thin circle. Cut each circle in half.
  8. Take one half and form a cone. Fill the cone with the potato and pea mixture and seal the top. Repeat with the remaining dough and filling.
  9. Fry the samosas in hot oil until golden brown, about 3-5 minutes. Drain on paper towels and serve hot with your favorite chutney or sauce.

Enjoy!
