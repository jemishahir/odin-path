Wings


Here is a recipe for classic buffalo wings:

    Ingredients:
  • 2 1/2 pounds chicken wings, tips removed, drumettes and flats separated
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup hot sauce (such as Frank's RedHot)

Directions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (205°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, mix together the flour, paprika, garlic powder, salt, cayenne pepper, and black pepper.
  3. Add the chicken wings to the bowl and toss to coat evenly with the flour mixture.
  4. lace the wings on the prepared baking sheet in a single layer. Bake for 45-50 minutes, flipping the wings over halfway through, until they are crispy and golden brown.
  5. While the wings are baking, melt the butter in a small saucepan over medium heat. Add the hot sauce and stir to combine.
  6. When the wings are done, transfer them to a large bowl and pour the hot sauce mixture over them. Toss the wings to coat them evenly with the sauce.
  7. Serve hot with celery sticks and blue cheese dressing, if desired.

Enjoy!
